Introduction

Nadol Lueprapai is currently a high school student, studying computer science. He is fluent in both English and Thai and has many interests.

Among the programming languages he has learned are HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Lua (Roblox), and Python. Many of which were learnt through self-study and online resources.

He also has a background in public speaking through his involvement in Model United Nations (MUN) conferences. As a result, he is familiar with political concepts and contemporary issues. He has also participated in debate competitions, such as the World Scholar's Cup hosted at Yale University.

In his free time, he enjoys reading, video games, exercise and culinary delights.

He is currently based in Bangkok, Thailand, and is open to opportunities for collaboration and learning.
